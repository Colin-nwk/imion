KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT

The objective of knowledge management is to concretise the process of identifying, organising, storing, and disseminating maritime policy and strategies within the national maritime ecosystem and beyond. Essentially this would be aimed at capturing, sharing and promoting the effective usage of maritime hindsight, insights and foresight in a coordinated and productive manner.

CAPACITY BUILDING

The objective of capacity building is to develop and strengthen the skills, abilities, instincts and resources that maritime industries in the GoG require to survive, adapt and thrive in maritime-related duties, especially in this fast-changing world. Pointedly, the essence is for an improvement in the maritime policy landscape towards building collective abilities to be more productive and perform better, through learning, adaptation and applicability.
