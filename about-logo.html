ELEMENTS OF OUR LOGO

MEANING OF SYMBOLS

The Wheel - A ship’s wheel, in spite of the size of a ship, is what enables the ship to follow a desired direction. Accordingly, The Wheel in the IMION logo signifies ‘moving along the pathway of maritime knowledge towards prosperity’.

The Gold Circles - The Gold Circles symbolise the Institute’s ‘multidimensional and interdisciplinary approach to learning’ about the golden opportunities that abound in the blue economy.

The Telescope - The Telescope in the logo serves as an inspiration for the Institutes ‘vision for advancing blue prosperity in Nigeria and beyond’.

The Pen - The Pen in the logo is to motivate the Institute to always reckon with the ‘power of change through research, innovation and advocacy’.
